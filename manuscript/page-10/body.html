Dedication

TO,

HARRIET BEECHER STOWE:

WHOSE NAME, THROUGHOUT THE WORLD, IS IDENTIFIED WITH THE GREAT REFORM:

THIS NARRATIVE, AFFORDING ANOTHER

'Key to Uncle Tom's Cabin,

IS RESPECTFULLY DEDICATED.
